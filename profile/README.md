## What we stand for

- **Data sovereignty.** Your data stays yours. We design systems where you own and control the models, embeddings, and pipelines that touch it — not a vendor whose pricing or policies can change next quarter.
- **Privacy by default.** No surprise telemetry, no quiet retention, no third-party training on your inputs.
- **Public Benefit Corporation.** Chartered to balance shareholder return with long-term public benefit. Those commitments are in our governing documents, not just our marketing.
- **Growing Organic Software.** We build systems that adapt as your business changes, instead of brittle deliverables that calcify on day one.
